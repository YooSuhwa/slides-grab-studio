Issues and Mitigation Strategies

Supply Chain Disruption
The reliance on multiple suppliers for components and materials poses a risk of supply chain disruption.
Diversify sourcing options and implementing contingency plans to address supply chain disruptions promptly.
Technical Challenges
The complexity of developing smart home appliances presents technical challenges that could impact product quality.
Collaborate closely with technical experts, and allocate sufficient resources for troubleshooting and problem-solving.
Market Competition
The smart home appliance market is highly competitive, with established brands and new entrants vying for market share.
Differentiate offerings through unique value propositions, and develop robust marketing strategies to position effectively.
