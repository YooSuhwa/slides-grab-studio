Voice of Customer Process

A four-step loop from listening to action

Designed for process overviews, methodologies, and operating models with four evenly weighted stages.

1

Listen

Collect customer feedback from interactions, surveys, tickets, social channels, and field notes.

2

Understand

Analyze patterns to identify pain points, demand clusters, and emerging opportunities.

3

Act

Prioritize what matters most and connect those insights to product and service improvements.

4

Respond

Update customers and internal teams on what changed, why it changed, and what comes next.
