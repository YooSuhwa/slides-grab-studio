# Section Divider

A breather between topics. Signals "we're moving to something new."

## Principles

**This is a pause, not content.** It lets the audience reset between sections. Think of it as a chapter title page in a book.

**Treat it like a mini cover.** One section title. Maybe a short subtitle. That's all. Bold and spacious, or quiet and minimal — follow the pack mood.

## Variants

- Left-aligned: same position as cover — consistency
- Centered: emphasizes the transition
- Bottom-anchored inverted: subtitle above, title below — breaks rhythm intentionally

## Avoid

- Body text or lists on a section divider
- Same density as a content slide (emptiness IS the point)
