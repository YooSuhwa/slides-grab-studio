# Highlight Slide

Emphasizes one key insight or message. "Remember this one thing."

## Principles

**Say one thing only.** The purpose is a single takeaway. Multiple points make it a content slide, not a highlight.

**The title IS the message.** The title itself should carry the insight. Body text just briefly elaborates.

**Whitespace amplifies.** Less content means more empty space, which draws the eye to what remains.

## Structure

A left-right split works well: big keyword/title on the left, short explanation on the right. Or use the full slide for one large statement centered.

## Avoid

- More than 2-3 elements (it's a highlight, not a dashboard)
- Long body text (keep it short)
- Treating it as a content slide with fewer words
